Our Story

Founded in 1998 by educators and child development specialists, ABC Toys Group began with a simple mission: to create toys that are not only fun but also contribute to a child's growth and development.

Over the past {{ stats.years_in_business }} years, we've grown from a small workshop to an international brand, but our commitment to quality and educational value remains unchanged.
